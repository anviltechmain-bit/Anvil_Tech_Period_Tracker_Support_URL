Privacy Policy

Anvil Period Tracker is designed as an offline period tracking app. The app stores your entries on your device. It does not use Firebase, cloud sync, or third-party analytics SDKs.

What the app stores

  • Period tracking entries, symptoms, sleep details, and activity selections you save in the app.
  • Cycle settings and local app preferences needed to keep the app working.
  • CSV backups only when you choose to export them.

Where data is stored

  • Saved locally inside app storage on the device.
  • Encrypted app storage is used when supported by the device.
  • Device backup and cleartext traffic are disabled in the Android app configuration.

What the app does not do

  • No account creation.
  • No cloud sync.
  • No Firebase services.
  • No third-party analytics or behavior tracking SDKs.

Ads

  • The app uses Google Mobile Ads to show one pre-muted interstitial ad about 5 seconds after opening.
  • The interstitial is limited to no more than once every 7 days.
  • Ad loading may involve Google services and ad-related device information as part of Google Mobile Ads delivery.

Permissions and device behavior

  • Notifications may be requested so the app can send a daily reminder at 8:00 PM.
  • Screenshots are intentionally allowed.
  • You control CSV import and export manually.

Support

  • Your device model and Android version.
  • What screen you were on when the problem happened.
  • Whether you were importing CSV, using notifications, or viewing the history graph.

Support scope

  • App help, bug reports, CSV backup questions, and basic setup issues.
  • General feature clarification.
  • Not medical diagnosis or emergency care.

Terms and App Notice

Anvil Period Tracker is provided for personal cycle tracking and general education. It is not a medical device, diagnosis tool, or emergency service.

  • Use of the app is at your own discretion.
  • You are responsible for reviewing exported CSV files and managing your own backups.
  • The app is intended to help with personal record keeping, not to replace professional medical advice.
  • Use must follow your local laws, platform rules, and clinic guidance where relevant.

By continuing to use the app after accepting the in-app notice, you accept the app terms, privacy summary, and ad disclosure.

Health Notice

The educational content inside the app gives a general overview of menstrual phases and common symptoms. Bodies vary, and not everyone will have the same cycle length or experience the same symptoms.

  • Seek medical care for severe pain, very heavy bleeding, fainting, or sudden major cycle changes.
  • Ask a clinician if cycles stay shorter than 24 days or longer than 38 days over time.
  • Ask a clinician if periods stop for 90 days when not pregnant, breastfeeding, or under direct medical guidance.
